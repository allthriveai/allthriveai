## ðŸŽ¨ Implementation Notes

### Quest Difficulty Guidelines

**Easy Quests (10-50 XP)**
- Can be completed in 1-2 actions
- No prerequisites required
- Introduce users to basic features
- Examples: First login, first quiz, first comment

**Medium Quests (60-150 XP)**
- Require 3-10 actions or consistent effort
- May require some skill or knowledge
- Encourage regular platform use
- Examples: 5-day streak, complete 10 quizzes

**Hard Quests (160-400 XP)**
- Require significant effort or skill
- May take days or weeks to complete
- Demonstrate mastery or dedication
- Examples: 30-day streak, 25 projects, perfect scores

**Epic Quests (450-1000 XP)**
- Long-term commitments (weeks to months)
- Require exceptional dedication
- Platform milestones and major achievements
- Examples: 100 quizzes, 50-day streak, all topics mastered

## ðŸ“Š Quest Balance Guidelines

### XP Distribution
- **Daily quests**: 20-100 XP (can be repeated)
- **Weekly quests**: 100-300 XP
- **Monthly quests**: 300-600 XP
- **One-time achievements**: 50-1000 XP based on difficulty

### Quest Availability
- **Evergreen**: Always available, can be completed anytime
- **Recurring**: Reset daily, weekly, or monthly
- **Time-Limited**: Available for specific dates/events
- **Sequential**: Unlock after completing prerequisites

### Difficulty Distribution
- **Easy**: 40% of quests (onboarding, first-time actions)
- **Medium**: 35% of quests (regular engagement)
- **Hard**: 20% of quests (dedication, consistency)
- **Epic**: 5% of quests (long-term achievements)

## ðŸŽ¯ Quest Design Principles

1. **Clear Objectives**: Quest description should clearly state what to do
2. **Meaningful Rewards**: XP should match effort and difficulty
3. **Progressive Challenge**: Easier quests lead to harder versions
4. **Diverse Activities**: Encourage exploration of all platform features
5. **Time Respect**: Don't require excessive grinding
6. **Social Balance**: Mix individual and community-focused quests
7. **Accessibility**: Quests for all skill levels and time commitments
8. **Creativity Encouragement**: Reward quality over quantity where possible

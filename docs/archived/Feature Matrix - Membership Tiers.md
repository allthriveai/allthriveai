## Pricing & Billing

### Price Points
- **Community Pro**: $18/month ($54/quarter, $216/year)
- **Pro Learn**: $35/month ($105/quarter, $420/year)
### 7-Day Free Trial
- Available for Community Pro tier only
- No payment method required
- Full Pro features unlocked during trial
- Automatically reverts to Free after trial unless subscribed
- One trial per user (lifetime)
### Billing Options
- **Quarterly** (MVP): Primary billing cycle to reduce churn
- **Annual**: 10-15% discount
- **Monthly** (Post-launch): For users who prefer flexibility

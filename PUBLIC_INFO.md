# AllThrive AI - AI Portfolio Platform with Gamified Learning & Discovery

## Overview

AllThrive AI is an AI portfolio platform that combines project showcasing, gamified learning, and community discovery. It's designed for AI enthusiasts, developers, researchers, and learners to grow their skills, share their work, and connect with others in the AI community.

## What is AllThrive AI?

AllThrive AI is a comprehensive platform where users can:

- **Showcase AI Projects**: Build and display portfolios of AI-powered projects with rich documentation, code examples, and live demos
- **Learn Through Gamification**: Progress through interactive challenges, earn achievements, and level up skills with structured learning paths
- **Discover & Explore**: Browse innovative AI projects, tools, and techniques from the community
- **Connect & Collaborate**: Engage with fellow AI practitioners, get feedback, and participate in collaborative learning

## API Access

AllThrive AI provides a RESTful API for programmatic access:
- `/api/v1/projects/` - Project management
- `/api/v1/conversations/` - AI chat interactions
- `/api/v1/users/` - User profiles
- `/api/v1/tools/` - AI tool directory
